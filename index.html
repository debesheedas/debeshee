Hello, I'm Debeshee Das

I'm a second-year Master's student in Computer Science at ETH Zurich, specializing in AI safety and security research. My work focuses on understanding and mitigating vulnerabilities in foundation models and AI systems, with particular emphasis on adversarial robustness and privacy-preserving machine learning.
